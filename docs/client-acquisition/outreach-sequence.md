# 📧 Outreach Sequence — Ready-to-Send Templates

| Day | Action | Channel | Goal |
|-----|--------|---------|------|
| 1 | Connection request | LinkedIn | Get accepted |
| 2 | Value message | LinkedIn DM | Show expertise |
| 7 | Cold email | Email | Offer free audit |
| 14 | Follow-up | Email | Close or nurture |
