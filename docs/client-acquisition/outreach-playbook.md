# 📞 Client Acquisition Playbook

## Channel 1: LinkedIn (40% of deals)

### Step 1: Build Target List (Day 15)
- Search LinkedIn for: "founder", "CEO", "operations manager"
- Filter by: e-commerce, agencies, SaaS (10-200 employees)
- Save 50 prospects in Google Sheets

### Step 2: Connection Requests (Days 16-20)
**Template:**
```
Hi [Name], I noticed [specific thing about their business]. I help [niche] automate [task]. Worth connecting?
```
- Send 10/day
- Goal: 30-40 accepted

### Step 3: Engage (Days 16-20)
- Like + comment on 5 posts/day from new connections
- Add value, not generic comments
- Goal: Build relationship before selling

### Step 4: Value-First Messages (Days 21-25)
**Template:**
```
Hi [Name],

I've been following your posts on [topic] - really insightful.

I work with [niche] to automate [specific task], typically saving 15-20 hours/week.

Quick question: How much time does your team spend on [task] weekly?

Would be happy to share some ideas if relevant.

[Your name]
```
- Goal: 5-10 positive responses

### Step 5: Discovery Calls (Days 26-30)
- Send Calendly link to positive responders
- Goal: 3-5 calls

## Channel 2: Cold Email (30% of deals)

### Email Sequence
| Email | Day | Subject | Goal |
|-------|-----|---------|------|
| 1 | 16-17 | "Quick question about [problem]" | 10-15% response |
| 2 | 19-20 | "How [similar company] saves 15 hrs/wk" | 10-15% response |
| 3 | 22-23 | "3 ways to cut [task] time in half" | 10-15% response |
| 4 | 24-25 | "Following up on my earlier message" | 5-10% response |

### Tools (all free)
- Hunter.io free tier: 50 searches/month
- Gmail: Send emails
- Google Sheets: Track responses

## Channel 3: Social Media (20% of deals)

### Twitter/X (30 min/day)
- Post 1 daily tip about AI automation
- Engage with 5-10 relevant tweets
- Reply to people asking about automation

### Reddit (3x/week)
- Subreddits: r/ecommerce, r/Entrepreneur, r/freelance, r/smallbusiness
- Answer questions, be helpful, not salesy
- Mention services only when relevant

## Channel 4: Referrals (10% of deals)

- Email 20 people you know
- Offer: "$200 credit for any successful referral"
- Ask for 5 warm introductions

## Discovery Call Script (15 min)

```
"Hi [Name], thanks for jumping on. I'll keep this brief.

I work with [niche] to automate [specific task], typically saving 15-20 hours/week.

Before I dive in — what's your biggest challenge with [task] right now?"

[Listen 2-3 minutes]

"That makes total sense. I worked with [similar company] who had the same issue.

Here's what we did: [brief explanation]

Result: [specific benefit] in [timeframe].

Does that sound valuable for your business?"

[If yes] "Here's what I'm thinking... [propose solution]. Does that work?"

[If yes] "My typical process: [timeline + pricing]. Sound good?"
```

## Metrics to Track

| Metric | Daily Target | Tool |
|--------|-------------|------|
