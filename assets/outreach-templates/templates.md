```
Day 1: Email 1 (I analyzed your business)

## Outreach Targeting

### Ideal Client Profile:
```
Company Size: 10-200 employees (too small = no budget, too big = slow sales)
Industry Focus (Priority Order):
  1. E-commerce / DTC brands (high volume, repetitive tasks)
  2. SaaS companies (tech-savvy, understand AI value)
  3. Professional services (law firms, accounting, consulting)
  4. Real estate (document-heavy, lead-driven)
  5. Healthcare admin (appointment scheduling, documentation)

Target Titles:
  - Founder / CEO
  - Head of Operations
  - VP Sales / Head of Sales
  - Customer Success Manager
  - Office Manager

Geographic Focus (Priority):
  1. United States (willing to pay, understand AI value)
  2. UAE / Saudi Arabia (growing, tech-forward)
  3. UK / Europe
  4. Canada
```

### Where to Find Leads:
```
1. LinkedIn Sales Navigator (free trial → cancel)
2. Google Maps (local businesses)
3. Industry directories
4. Reddit forums (r/smallbusiness, r/entrepreneur, r/ecommerce)
5. Indie Hackers community
6. Product Hunt (companies launching products)
7. Twitter/X (search for pain point keywords)
```

[Link to onboarding form]

[Calendar link or "Just suggest a time"]

### When They Say "How Much?":
```
Our packages range from $2,000 to $10,000/month depending on the complexity and volume.

But rather than dump pricing on you, I'd rather understand what you need first.

Every business is different — some need a simple email agent, others need a multi-agent workflow.

Let's do the free audit first. I'll give you a specific price based on what makes sense for you.

Does that work?
```

### When They Say "Not Interested":
```
No problem at all!

If anything changes or if you know someone who might benefit, I'd appreciate the referral.

Either way — good luck with [Company name]! 🚀
```

```
I understand — it's an investment.

But let me ask: How much is [the problem] costing you right now?

If your team is spending 15+ hours/week on [specific task], that's roughly $[X] in salary costs alone.

Our agent typically costs less than 1/3 of that, and it works 24/7 without breaks or mistakes.

Want me to build a quick ROI breakdown specific to your situation? It might make the decision easier.
