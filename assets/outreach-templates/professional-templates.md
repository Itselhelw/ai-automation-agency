## Outreach Cadence (Best Practice)

### LinkedIn Sequence:
| Day | Action | Template |
|-----|--------|----------|
| 1 | Connection Request | Short & direct |
| 3 | Follow-Up #1 | Value + observation |
| 7 | Follow-Up #2 | Case study / video |
| 14 | Follow-Up #3 | Breakup |

### Cold Email Sequence:
| Day | Action | Template |
|-----|--------|----------|
| 1 | Email 1 | Specific observation |
| 4 | Email 2 | Free audit offer |
| 8 | Email 3 | Social proof / case study |
| 14 | Email 4 | Breakup |

**Rules:**
- Never send more than 4 emails without a reply
- Always provide value in every touchpoint
- Personalize at least ONE thing per email
- Keep emails under 150 words
- Always include a clear, low-friction CTA

## Lead Scoring Framework

| Signal | Score | Meaning |
|--------|-------|---------|
| Filled out form | +10 | High intent |
| Replied to email | +8 | Interested |
| Booked a call | +15 | Very high intent |
| Sent "not interested" | -5 | Disqualified |
| No reply after 4 touches | -3 | Cold lead |
| Referred someone else | +12 | Advocate |

**Hot Lead (15+):** Prioritize — book call immediately  
**Warm Lead (5-14):** Nurture — send value content  
**Cold Lead (<5):** Automated sequence only
