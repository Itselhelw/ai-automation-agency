# Email Outreach Templates

## Template 1: Problem-Focused
**Subject:** Quick question about [problem]

```
Hi [Name],

I work with [niche] to solve [specific problem].

Most [niche] I talk to spend [X hours/week] on [task].

Quick question: Is this something your team deals with?

[Your name]
```

## Template 2: Solution-Focused
**Subject:** How [similar company] saves 15 hours/week

```
Hi [Name],

I recently helped a company similar to yours automate [task].

They went from [problem] to [result] in 2 weeks.

Thought you might find it interesting.

[Your name]
```
